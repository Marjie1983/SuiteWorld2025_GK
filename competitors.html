⚔️ Competitor & Partner Talk Tracks

Strategic Partners

For Tipalti Users

Opening: "Tipalti is excellent for payment automation - we're partners. How are you ensuring those efficient payments go to compliant, low-risk vendors?"

Value Prop: "Tipalti ensures vendors get paid accurately. We ensure you're paying the RIGHT vendors for the RIGHT reasons by adding risk and contract intelligence upfront."

For Airbase Users

Opening: "Great choice with Airbase for spend management - we partner closely. How are you assessing the vendors behind that spend?"

Value Prop: "Airbase manages spend workflows. We manage the vendor relationships behind them. Together, it's complete spend intelligence."

Direct Competitors

vs. Zip

Counter: "Zip offers 50 AI agents for tasks, which is impressive. But we provide an intelligent ecosystem. While they automate intake, we're connecting NetSuite, Tipalti, and Airbase into one Vendor Command Center that can see risk, contracts, and spend together."

vs. Coupa

Counter: "Coupa has an $8T data moat, but it's blind to your specific contract terms and vendor risks. We leverage your existing ecosystem and get you live in 6 weeks, not 9-12 months."

Other Key Competitors

vs. CLM-only (Icertis, Ironclad)

Counter: "A dedicated CLM is great, but it creates another silo. We offer a unified platform where your contracts are connected directly to vendor risk profiles and live spend data from NetSuite, which a CLM-only tool can't do."

vs. Risk-only (OneTrust, Aravo)

Counter: "A risk platform tells you if a vendor is risky, but it doesn't stop procurement from buying from them. We are a procurement-first platform that puts risk intelligence directly into the workflow, ensuring compliance before a single dollar is spent."
