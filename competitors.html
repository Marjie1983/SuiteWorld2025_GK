ü§ù Our Partnership Philosophy

Gatekeeper completes the NetSuite ecosystem. We provide the critical risk, contract, and compliance intelligence that other financial automation providers don't. We partner with best-in-class solutions to deliver the complete source-to-pay ecosystem NetSuite customers need.

Strategic Partners

Tipalti

Their Excellence: World-class AP automation and global payment capabilities. Named NetSuite's 2024 Growth Partner of the Year.

What They Don't Provide: Pre-payment vendor screening, contract lifecycle management, or comprehensive risk assessment.

Our Synergy: We add the upfront vendor risk screening and connect contracts to payments, ensuring Tipalti pays the right, compliant vendors.

Airbase

Their Excellence: Excellent spend management platform with corporate card integration and automated approval workflows.

What They Don't Provide: Vendor risk assessment, contract management, or compliance monitoring.

Our Synergy: We enable risk-aware spending decisions and link all spend directly to contract terms, ensuring secure and compliant vendor relationships.

Zone & Co

Their Excellence: Platinum sponsor with advanced financial automation and quote-to-cash expertise.

What They Don't Provide: Vendor management, risk assessment tools, or contract lifecycle features.

Our Synergy: We add the entire vendor lifecycle management piece, providing the contract and risk intelligence needed for truly informed financial operations.

Direct Competitors

Zip

Their Strengths: Industry-leading intake management and "Built for NetSuite" status.

Their Gaps: Limited risk assessment, no comprehensive CLM, and lacks continuous monitoring.

Our Advantage: We offer a complete, unified platform for CLM, TPRM, and Spend, all powered by LuminIQ intelligence.

Coupa

Their Strengths: Market leader in Business Spend Management (BSM) with a strong supplier network.

Their Gaps: Complex implementation, limited contract lifecycle features, and risk is an afterthought.

Our Advantage: A risk-first approach, rapid deployment, and an open partner ecosystem model.

Medius

Their Strengths: Gold Sponsor at SuiteWorld with strong AI-powered AP automation.

Their Gaps: Invoice-centric only, no risk management, and no pre-signature contract capabilities.

Our Advantage: We manage the full contracting lifecycle and provide strategic vendor management, not just invoice processing.

Ivalua

Their Strengths: Unified source-to-pay platform with a large supplier network.

Their Gaps: Complex implementation, closed platform, and enterprise-level pricing.

Our Advantage: We are a partner-friendly platform with a faster time to value and a focus on the mid-market.

Other Key Competitors (Not Sponsoring)

Icertis & Ironclad

Their Strengths: They are strong leaders in the CLM-only space.

Their Gaps: They have a narrow focus on just contracts, with no spend management, weak vendor risk, and no partner ecosystem for NetSuite.

Our Advantage: We offer a unified platform approach that connects Risk, Contracts, and Spend. We are NetSuite-optimized and integrate with the partners you already use.
