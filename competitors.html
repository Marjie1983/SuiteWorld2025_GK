ü§ù Our Partnership Philosophy

Gatekeeper completes the NetSuite ecosystem. We provide the critical risk, contract, and compliance intelligence that other financial automation providers don't. We partner with best-in-class solutions to deliver the complete source-to-pay ecosystem NetSuite customers need.

vs. Zip (Direct Competitor)

Their Strengths:

  • Industry-leading intake management
  • "Built for NetSuite" verified status

Their Gaps:

  • Limited risk assessment capabilities
  • No comprehensive Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM)

Gatekeeper's Advantage:

  • LuminIQ provides unified intelligence across the entire vendor lifecycle.
  • We offer a complete, integrated platform for CLM, Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM), and Spend.

vs. Coupa (Direct Competitor)

Their Strengths:

  • Market leader in Business Spend Management (BSM)
  • Comprehensive spend management features

Their Gaps:

  • Notoriously long and complex implementation process
  • Risk management is often an afterthought in their platform

Gatekeeper's Advantage:

  • Our risk-first approach prevents issues, it doesn't just record them.
  • We are built on an open partner ecosystem, not a closed platform.

vs. Medius (Direct Competitor)

Their Strengths:

  • Gold Sponsor status at SuiteWorld
  • Strong AI-powered AP automation

Their Gaps:

  • Overly focused on invoices, missing the bigger picture
  • No vendor risk management capabilities

Gatekeeper's Advantage:

  • We manage the full contracting lifecycle, not just the invoice.
  • Our process includes upfront vendor screening to ensure compliance.

vs. NetSuite Native (SuiteProcurement)

Its Strengths:

  • Natively built into the NetSuite ERP
  • Includes AI-powered Bill Capture

Its Gaps:

  • No vendor risk assessment features
  • No CLM capabilities whatsoever

Gatekeeper's Advantage:

  • We provide the complete risk and contract intelligence NetSuite lacks.
  • Our platform is designed to work with the entire partner ecosystem.
