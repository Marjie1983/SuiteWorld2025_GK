üîç Discovery Questions

General Discovery

Opening Questions

"What vendor-related fire drill pulled you away from strategic work this week?"

Gets them talking about real pain immediately.

"If you could see one thing about a vendor before approving any transaction, what would it be?"

Reveals their primary concern (risk, spend, compliance, performance).

"What's the most expensive vendor surprise you've had in the last 12 months?"

Uncovers specific pain around auto-renewals, compliance failures, or overspend.

"How do you know if you're paying two different vendors for the same service?"

Highlights duplicate spend and vendor sprawl issues.

Situational/Trigger Discovery

  • For Recent M&A: "How are you handling vendor consolidation post-merger?"
  • For Rapid Growth: "Your vendor count doubled last year - how has your management process scaled?"
  • For Audit Preparation: "What vendor documentation are the auditors asking for that's hard to produce?"
  • For Cost Cutting Initiatives: "If you had to cut vendor spend by 20% tomorrow, how would you identify where?"

üí° Power Questions for Champions

"Who else needs to be convinced this is a problem worth solving?"

Identifies the buying committee.

"If you do nothing, what happens?"

Creates urgency.

For NetSuite Admins

"How many custom fields did you create to track vendor compliance?"
"How many hours per week does your team spend on vendor data hygiene?"

Quantifies the manual effort pain.

"What happens when Legal needs contract data that's not in NetSuite?"

Exposes integration gaps.

"How do you handle vendor onboarding when different departments have different requirements?"

Reveals process fragmentation.

"What vendor data do you wish NetSuite had that it doesn't?"

Opens door to risk scores, contract terms, compliance status.

For Finance

"Can Bill Capture tell you if charges exceed contract rates?"
"How often do you pay for something only to find out later the contract had already expired?"

Uncovers contract visibility gaps.

"What percentage of your vendor spend is with companies you've never risk-assessed?"

Quantifies risk exposure.

"How do you currently track spend against contract commitments or volume discounts?"

Reveals contract intelligence gaps.

"If a vendor invoice is 20% higher than last month, how long before you know why?"

Tests their spend intelligence capabilities.

For Procurement

"Does SuiteProcurement check vendor risk before creating POs?"
"How many vendors get onboarded outside your official process?"

Reveals shadow procurement.

"How do you know if you already have a similar solution from another vendor?"
"Walk me through what happens when a vendor fails a risk assessment today."

Process maturity check.

"What's your average time from vendor identification to first PO?"

Baseline metric for improvement.

For Risk/Compliance

"How do you stop high-risk vendors from entering SuiteProcurement?"
"What happens when a vendor's risk profile changes AFTER they're onboarded?"

Continuous monitoring gap.

"How do you ensure contract terms match the vendor's risk level?"
"Show me how you'd find all vendors with access to customer data right now."

Tests vendor categorization and reporting.

"How do you track vendor certificates, insurance, and attestations?"

Document management pain.

For Legal

"How do SuiteProcurement POs reference contract terms?"
"How many contracts are on auto-renewal that shouldn't be?"

Reveals monitoring gaps.

"What's your current contract repository and how does it connect to NetSuite?"

Integration pain.

"How long does it take to answer 'what do we spend with this vendor across all contracts?'"

Tests connected intelligence.

"What happens when a vendor invoice references a contract that Legal can't find?"
