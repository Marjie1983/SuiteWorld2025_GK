üîç Discovery Questions

General Discovery

Opening Questions

"What's your current vendor management tech stack?"
"How well do these systems talk to each other?"
"Where do you spend the most time on manual reconciliation?"

Enhanced Discovery

"What vendor-related fire drill pulled you away from strategic work this week?"

Gets them talking about real pain immediately.

"What's the most expensive vendor surprise you've had in the last 12 months?"

Uncovers specific pain around auto-renewals, compliance failures, or overspend.

For NetSuite Admins

"How many custom fields did you create to track vendor compliance?"
"What happens when Legal needs contract data that's not in NetSuite?"

Exposes integration gaps.

For Finance

"Can Bill Capture tell you if charges exceed contract rates?"
"What percentage of your vendor spend is with companies you've never risk-assessed?"

Quantifies risk exposure.

For Procurement

"Does SuiteProcurement check vendor risk before creating POs?"
"How many vendors get onboarded outside your official process?"

Reveals shadow procurement.

For Risk/Compliance

"How do you stop high-risk vendors from entering SuiteProcurement?"
"What happens when a vendor's risk profile changes AFTER they're onboarded?"

Continuous monitoring gap.

For Legal

"How do SuiteProcurement POs reference contract terms?"
"How many contracts are on auto-renewal that shouldn't be?"

Reveals monitoring gaps.

For the C-Suite

"Beyond the invoice price, where do you see the most financial leakage in your vendor relationships today‚Äîis it in missed renewals, non-compliant spend, or something else?"

Directly addresses cost reduction and waste.

"When your board asks about third-party risk, what are the one or two metrics you wish you could provide them instantly?"

Speaks to their need for board-level reporting and quantifiable risk.

"You've made a significant investment in NetSuite as your financial core. How are you ensuring that investment is protected from the risks introduced by your vendors?"

Positions Gatekeeper as an enhancement to their existing strategy.

"If we could propose a project that was self-funding, paid for by eliminating waste in your current vendor spend, would that be a priority for you this year?"

Frames the discussion around investment and ROI, not cost.
