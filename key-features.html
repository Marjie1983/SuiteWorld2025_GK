High-Impact Use Cases

Financial Compliance: The W8 Form Review Process

For new vendors, Gatekeeper captures critical financial compliance documents like the W8 Form directly through the Vendor Portal. A Lumin Agent can then review the document against customizable standards and approve the vendor's financial compliance in seconds, not hours.

 

Data Validation: The Automated TIN/VAT Checks

Ensure financial integrity from day one. During onboarding, our workflow provides real-time tax ID verification (TIN/VAT checks) to prevent fraud and ensure data accuracy before a vendor is ever synced to NetSuite.

 

Risk Mitigation: How Lumin Agents Flag Issues

Embed due diligence directly into your onboarding workflow. Lumin Agents can automatically review vendor submissions, flag risks based on your custom standards, and route vendors to an internal risk review, ensuring no high-risk vendors are ever approved.
