A Single Source of Truth

Our integration transforms vendor and contract management by embedding a dedicated **"Gatekeeper" tab** directly within your NetSuite vendor records. This provides immediate, context-rich access to all associated contracts, risk data, and compliance information without ever leaving the NetSuite UI.

Key Message: "Stop juggling multiple systems. We bring the vendor and contract intelligence you need directly into the NetSuite environment you already use."

 

How the Data Syncs

Vendor Data Sync (Gatekeeper â†’ NetSuite)

Gatekeeper controls data quality. New vendors are onboarded, risk-assessed, and validated in Gatekeeper first. Only once they meet your standards are they synced to NetSuite, ensuring your vendor master remains clean and accurate.

Spend Data Sync (NetSuite â†’ Gatekeeper)

When you create and pay a bill in NetSuite, you can associate it with a Gatekeeper contract. NetSuite then automatically pushes the expenditure data to Gatekeeper as 'Spend,' maintaining the original NetSuite reference IDs for perfect traceability.

Bi-Directional Linking

The integration is seamless in both directions. The Gatekeeper tab in NetSuite links directly to the full records in Gatekeeper. Conversely, vendor and contract records in Gatekeeper include a hyperlink that takes you directly back to the corresponding record in NetSuite, so you never lose context.
