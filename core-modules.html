ğŸ“¦ Core Modules Overview

Vendor Management

Seamlessly integrated with NetSuite, Gatekeeper enhances your existing vendor records. We create a single source of truth by providing structured workflows to manage the entire vendor lifecycle, from intake and onboarding to performance and offboarding.

  • Centralised Vendor Data
  • Automated Onboarding Workflows
  • Vendor Portal for Collaboration

Contract Management

Gatekeeper provides a complete Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM) solution. Within any NetSuite vendor record, you can immediately access all associated contracts, view key metadata, and store all documents in a centralized, searchable repository.

  • Centralised Contract Repository
  • AI-Powered Data Extraction
  • Automated Renewal Alerts

Risk Management

Ensure financial integrity and protect your business by embedding risk management directly into your procurement process. We provide automated risk assessments for cybersecurity, financial stability, and tax ID verification, ensuring you only work with compliant, low-risk vendors.

  • Automated Due Diligence
  • Continuous Risk Monitoring
  • Audit-Ready Compliance Tracking

Spend Analytics

Connect your contracts to your financials. When you create a bill in NetSuite, the expenditure is automatically pushed to Gatekeeper, maintaining perfect traceability. This allows you to view detailed spend analysis and compare actuals against contract forecasts to identify variances instantly.

  • Automated Spend Tracking
  • Forecast vs. Actuals Reporting
  • Savings and ROI Dashboards
