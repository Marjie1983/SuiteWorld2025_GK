🏆 Customer Success Stories

Global SaaS Company: From Chaos to Command Center

The Problem:

They were drowning in manual work inside NetSuite, using spreadsheets to track vendor risk and contracts. With no visibility, they missed a critical auto-renewal on an underperforming software vendor, costing them over $250K.

The Gatekeeper Solution:

We unified their vendor data directly within their NetSuite instance, creating a single source of truth. Our AI-powered contract extraction and automated renewal workflows gave them the visibility they lacked.

The Quantifiable Result:

  • Identified **$1.2M in recoverable spend** in the first 6 months.
  • Reduced vendor onboarding time by **90%**.
  • Achieved **100% visibility** into contract renewals, preventing future unwanted costs.

Manufacturing Firm: Preventing Supply Chain Disruption

The Problem:

Their supply chain was exposed. A key supplier's insurance lapsed, and they only found out after an incident. This, combined with manual compliance tracking in NetSuite, put them at constant risk.

The Gatekeeper Solution:

We implemented automated, continuous compliance and risk monitoring. LuminIQ now automatically flags any vendor whose compliance status changes, sending alerts directly to the procurement team within their existing workflow.

The Quantifiable Result:

  • Prevented **3 major compliance breaches** in the first year.
  • Reduced audit preparation time by **80%**.
  • Increased supply chain resilience by ensuring **100% vendor compliance**.

Financial Services: Making NetSuite Audit-Ready

The Problem:

Preparing for audits was a manual, multi-week fire drill. They struggled to prove to auditors that their vendors in NetSuite met strict regulatory and security requirements.

The Gatekeeper Solution:

Gatekeeper created a centralized, audit-ready repository for all vendor documentation, risk assessments, and contract data, fully integrated with their NetSuite vendor records.

The Quantifiable Result:

  • Achieved a **"no findings"** result on their next vendor compliance audit.
  • Reduced the risk of regulatory fines, estimated at over **$500K**.
  • Freed up **hundreds of hours** of senior management time previously spent on audit prep.
