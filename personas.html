The Universal Truths for All Personas

Every SuiteWorld attendee shares these beliefs:

  • NetSuite is the center of their business universe.
  • They want solutions that enhance, not replace NetSuite.
  • They're overwhelmed by point solutions.
  • They want AI that prevents problems, not just processes transactions.

The Master Message: "NetSuite's 2025 SuiteProcurement handles transactions. Gatekeeper adds the intelligence - risk screening, contract management, and compliance monitoring. Together, they create complete vendor management."
